The Red Ribbon Club has been established in Shillong College in the year 2006 under National AIDS Control Programme (NACP).

YOUTH RED CROSS

Shillong College set up the Youth Red Cross Society on May 16th, 2012. Its primary aim is to develop among the students the spirit of humanitarian and social service through active and direct involvement in Red Cross activities.
